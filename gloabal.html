What causes climate change?And how does it relate to global warming?

Fossil fuels-coal, oil and gas - are by far the largest contributor to global climate change, accounting for ove 75 per cent of global greenhouse gas emissions and nearly 90 per cent of all carbon dioxide emissions. As greenhouse gas emissions blanket the Earth, they trap the sun's heat.
